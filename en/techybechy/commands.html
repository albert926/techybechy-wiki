How to use commands on Techy Bechy SMP

General:

/lobby Teleport to server lobby

/tpxp Teleport to SMP spawn using XP

/watchspawn Teleport to the spawn viewing platform, type again to return

 

Money & Economy:

/balance Checks current amount of money that you have

/pay Pay a player a certain amount of money

/sell Sell your item

 

Discord:

/discord Show discord server invite link

/link Link your discord account

/unlink Unlink your discord account

 

Parkour:

/pa leave Leave the current parkour

 

OneBlock:

/ob leave Leave OneBlock
